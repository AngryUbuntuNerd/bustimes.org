Apps

Although there’s no official bustimes.org app, I’m happy to recommend these competitors’ apps for your phone, tablet or watch.

{% load static %}

Citymapper

If you're lucky enough to live in London, Birmingham or Manchester, Citymapper is a much-loved app for journey planning and live times covering all modes of public transport.

Transit

Almost as nice as Citymapper. Covers London and Manchester.

UK Bus Checker

Covers the whole of Great Britain.

Stagecoach Bus

Offers live times and exact ticket prices for Stagecoach buses. (If you need to use any other company’s bus services, this app won’t help you.)
