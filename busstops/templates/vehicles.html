This is a live map of some of the buses in Norfolk. How cool is that?

How ‘live’?

I’m not sure. I fetch the information up to every 10 seconds, but the underlying data seems to change a bit less frequently than that.

Click on a bus to see when its position last changed.

Why only some?

I don’t know. Not all vehicles probably have the right satellite tracking equipment.

Only Simonds, Sanders, Lynx, Our Bus, West Norfolk Community Transport and First buses ever appear. I’ve noticed Sanders’ Coasthopper routes don’t appear, although Coasthopper-liveried buses do appear when they’re operating on other routes.
